ECOALARM

The first alarm that raise funds to save the environment.

EcoAlarm is the first alarm that wakes you up to the sounds of Argentina’s forests and, with Spotify, helps raise funds to save the environment. That means that when you wake up listening to nature, you’re helping to protect her, all without getting out of bed.
